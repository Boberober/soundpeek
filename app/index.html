soundpeek.js

created by robin björklund

Share your favorite songs.

What is it?

I love music (and Spotify). So I made this small dependency free JavaScript library that allows you to add previews of songs to your blog or website. Like this

How does it work?

I thought you'd never ask! With A Little Help From My Friends (Spotify). They make this possible with the help of their wonderful Web API.

So.. How do I use it?

It's super simple! Include the library at the bottom of your page and intitialize it.
Here's a simple example:

var soundpeek = new Soundpeek({
      targetSelector: '.soundpeek',
      prependElement: '<span class="play-button"></span>'
    });

Get track by text:

<span class="soundpeek">Bohemian Rhapsody</span>

Get track by Spotify URI:

<span class="soundpeek" data-uri="spotify:track:1AhDOtG9vPSOmsWgNW0BEY"></span>
